Top Products

Audit & Compliance Management (ACM)

Part of our configuration management stack, ACM is an integrated and comprehensive tool set that enables four core capabilities across the device life cycle within the network. The product is developed to cater for networks at telecom operators and multinational companies to provide:
Compliance & Remediation Management, Report Management, On-boarding & Inventory Management and Policy Management

Network Monitoring & Management (NMM)

Also part of the configuration management stack, NMS is an easy-to-use and affordable network monitoring solution that monitors network devices such as routers, switches, load balancers, controllers, firewalls, VMs, and servers with a future-proof design to accommodate the growth in IoT devices. Network monitoring is crucial for businesses that rely on networks for their operations.

Orchestration & Managed Services (OMS)

This generally covers business-critical services and functions that are essential to the business and it requires the operator’s ability to manage the client’s solution “end-to-end”, which entails offering self-service and active-monitoring features to the provided solution and proactively reporting faults and incidents, while ensuring that restoration happens within the committed SLAs. Our DevOps services for orchestration and managed services allow operators to compete with over-the-top suppliers. Our offerings in this domain span the areas of strategy, planning, acquisition, set-up, build, monitoring, and remediation to provide integrated SLAs for both cloud services and network services for a variety of applications from automation and orchestration to managed voice & data, LAN/WAN, Security, disaster recovery, mobility, and virtualization in DC & Cloud.
